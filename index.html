Help & Guide
1
Mount your phone on the dashboard
Use a phone mount so the front camera faces your face directly. Both eyes must be clearly visible. Avoid dark environments or harsh backlighting.
2
Calibrate EAR for your face
If you get too many false alarms, increase the EAR threshold in Settings. If drowsiness isn't caught, lower it. Default 0.40 suits most faces.
3
Emergency call — automatic, no tap needed
Go to Settings → Emergency Contact. Enter a name and number, set the SOS Call Timer (separate from the alarm timer), and toggle it on. When eyes stay closed past the SOS duration, your phone automatically calls — no tap required. Uses your own SIM, completely free.
4
GPS speed tracking
Allow location permission when prompted. The app reads your real GPS speed. Set your custom speed limit in Settings to log exceeded events.
5
Install as an app
In Chrome: tap the three-dot menu → "Add to Home Screen". On Safari (iPhone): tap Share → "Add to Home Screen". Works fully offline once installed.
